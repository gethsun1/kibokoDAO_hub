Join the Kiboko Revolution

KibokoDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization aimed at empowering Africa’s blockchain community by providing a collaborative platform that enhances education, financial inclusion, and decentralized innovation. Inspired by the Swahili term "Kiboko," meaning hippopotamus, which symbolizes strength and community, KibokoDAO seeks to bridge gaps in education, access, and resources across the continent.

What is KibokoDAO?

How do I vote?

Voting is simple! Just navigate to the Voting Page, select your proposal, and click your choice. Your voice matters!
